Book Description

Have you ever wondered where Earth's water came from? Or why leaves change color in the fall? Combining hilarious text and rigorous research, this book provides fun facts about animals, nature, science and more in an engaging and informative way for kids.

In their debut illustrated science book, MinuteEarth answers children's curious questions alongside engaging illustrations of the natural world.

MinuteEarth

MinuteEarth is a science education channel that has more than 2 million subscribers and over 350 million views on YouTube. The pun-loving scientists, writers, and illustrators who create MinuteEarth love to ponder all kinds of questions about our planet, no matter whether they are big or small, serious or silly. By digging through the scientific literature and talking to all sorts of experts, they create engaging and educational stories that inspire a love for science and an appreciation of our planet.
